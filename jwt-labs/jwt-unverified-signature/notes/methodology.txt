LAB 1 — Methodology
====================
1. Log in with low-privilege account — obtain valid JWT.
2. Decode payload: split on '.', base64-decode middle part.
3. Modify sub/role claim to privileged value (administrator, admin, true).
4. Re-encode payload (base64url, strip padding).
5. Replace middle part of token — keep original header and signature.
6. Send modified token — if 200 → signature never checked.

TOOLS: Burp JWT Editor, jwt_tool, manual base64
